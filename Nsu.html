NETAJI SUBHAS UNIVERSITY

Netaji Subhas University (NSU) is a private university located in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, established in 2018 under the Jharkhand State Private University Act. Here's what you need to know about the university:
University Highlights - Establishment Year: 2018 - Campus Size: 25 acres - Type: Private - Affiliated By: UGC, PCI, AICTE, NCTE, BCI, INC, JNRC, and NAAC grade 'A' - Courses: UG, PG, Diploma, Ph.D
Courses Offered - Popular Programs: BTech, MBA, BSc, BA - Other Courses: Management, Law, Arts and Humanities, Engineering, Pharmacy, Hotel Management, Nursing, Mass Communication - Certificate Course: Data Science in collaboration with Datamaze
Admissions - Admission Criteria: Merit-based or entrance-based - Application Mode: Online and offline - Scholarships: National Scholarship and E-Kalyan
Placements - Highest Package: INR 15 LPA - Average Package: INR 4.5 to 6.4 LPA - Top Recruiters: TCS, Deloitte, Just Dial, Airtel, Big Bazar, IDBI Federal, Flipkart, IndusInd Bank
Campus Facilities - Library: Central library with over 18,000 books, reports, journals, and online databases - Labs: Well-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Educational Technology - Hostel: Separate boys' and girls' hostels with essential facilities - Sports: Various sports facilities for students - Canteen: Healthy and tasty food options - Auditorium: Fully air-conditioned with latest audio-video system - Transport: Transportation facility for students and faculty
Rankings - India Today Ranking 2023: #237 out of 272 in India for MBA programs - NAAC Grade: A Overall, Netaji Subhas University offers a wide range of courses, excellent placement opportunities, and modern infrastructure, making it a great option for students in Jharkhand.
